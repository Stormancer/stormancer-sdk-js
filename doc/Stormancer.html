(static) PacketReliability :number

Different available reliability levels when sending a packet.

Type:
  • number
Properties:
Name Type Description
UNRELIABLE number

0 - The packet may be lost, or arrive out of order. There are no guarantees whatsoever.
UNRELIABLE_SEQUENCED number

1 - The packets arrive in order, but may be lost. If a packet arrives out of order, it is discarded. The last packet may also never arrive.
RELIABLE number

2 - The packets always reach destination, but may do so out of order.
RELIABLE_ORDERED number

3 - The packets always reach destination and in order.
RELIABLE_SEQUENCED number

4 - The packets arrive at destination in order. If a packet arrive out of order, it is ignored. That mean that packets may disappear, but the last one always reach destination.
